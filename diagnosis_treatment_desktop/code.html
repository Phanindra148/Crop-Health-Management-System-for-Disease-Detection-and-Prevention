info About this Condition

Phytophthora infestans is a highly destructive oomycete that affects tomatoes and potatoes. Given current humidity levels (82%), the pathogen is likely to spread rapidly across adjacent healthy leaves within 48 hours.

How to Cure

Recommended treatment plan by CropDoc AI

science ORGANIC FIRST
content_cut

Immediate Pruning

Remove all affected leaves and stems. Ensure you cut at least 2 inches into healthy tissue. DO NOT compost these clippings; burn or bag them immediately.

TIMING: ASAP TOOLS: STERILIZED
sanitizer

Copper Fungicide Application

Apply a liquid copper-based fungicide to the entire plant, focusing on the undersides of the leaves. This will create a protective barrier against further spore germination.

ORGANIC OPTION
water_drop

Modify Irrigation

Switch to drip irrigation if currently using overhead sprinklers. Keep foliage dry to prevent the humid microclimate that allows Blight to thrive.
