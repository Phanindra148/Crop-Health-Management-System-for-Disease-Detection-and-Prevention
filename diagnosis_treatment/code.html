How to Cure

1

Isolate Infected Plants

Remove and safely destroy all infected foliage. Do not compost these remains as spores can survive.

2

Apply Fungicide

Use a copper-based fungicide or products containing chlorothalonil. Apply thoroughly to both sides of the leaves.

3

Prune for Airflow

Thin out the lower branches to increase air circulation and reduce humidity around the plant base.
